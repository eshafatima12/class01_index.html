Nike is an American company that designs, manufactures, and sells athletic apparel, footwear, and sports equipment. It's the world's largest supplier of athletic apparel and shoes. Here are some facts about Nike: Founding: Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman co-founded Nike in 1964. Knight was a former track and field athlete, and Bowerman was his coach at the University of Oregon. Swoosh logo: Carolyn Davidson, a graphic design student at Portland State University, designed the Nike Swoosh in 1971. She was inspired by the wing of the Greek goddess of victory. Davidson was paid $35 for her work. "Just Do It" slogan: Nike's slogan originated from a convicted killer. Revenue: In 2022, Nike's revenue was over $46 billion.
